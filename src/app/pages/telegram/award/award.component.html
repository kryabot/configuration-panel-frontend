Telegram group awards
Awards are just labels which you (or group admins) can grant to your Telegram group to members. Award template are written in user information message by KryaBot (/who)
Award have counter which can be used in templates with: {{ '{' }}counter{{ '}' }}

Usage: To give award (as reply to user message): /award keyword
To create award: /setaward keyword template
To delete award: /deleteaward keyword
To get list of available award keywords: /getawards

Examples:
/setaward apple User have {{ '{' }}counter{{ '}' }} apples
/award apple
/award apple 10
