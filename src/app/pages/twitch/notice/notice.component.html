Chat notices {{alert.message}}
Notice help and explanations Notice is event occurance like subscribe, bits or raid in twitch chat which is visible to everyone. Here you can set up bot response to different type of notices. Also you can customize different responses depending of data count (amount of months, amount of bits) to avoid repetitive bot replies. Here comes your imagination!

If configuraton not found for received notice then bot will reply with default notice reaction: #user# PogChamp <3

IMPORTANT:
"Count from" and "Count to" is interval and they MUST NOT overlap for same notice type!
[0;10] and [5;15] is wrong
[0;10] and [10;15] is wrong
[0;10] and [11;15] is correct
[0;10] and [20;30] is correct

Available data tags:
#user# - nickname of person who initiates the notice
#giftreceiver# - nickname of person who receives gift subscribtion
#count# - count of amount:
Twitch bits - amount of bits
Subscribe, Twitch Prime - amount of months
Gift Subscribe (Mass), Gift Subscribe - total gifted count (if gifting person enabled setting to show total gift count)
Raid - amount of raiders
#promo# - Twitch promotion name. Only for Extending of subscribed gift type. Example: Subtember.
